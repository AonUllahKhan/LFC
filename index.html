John William Henry

OWNER

John William Henry is an American businessman and the founder of John W. Henry & Company. He is the principal owner of Liverpool Football Club and the Boston Red Sox.

Billy Hogan

CEO

Billy Hogan is a Chief Executive Officer of Liverpool Football Club. He was assigned for this position in 2017 and since then he is doing an excellent job for this historical Club Liverpool.

Jurgen Klopp

MANAGER

Jürgen Norbert Klopp is a German professional football manager and former player who is the manager of Premier League club Liverpool. He is widely regarded as one of the best managers in the world.

Zeljko Buvac

ASSISTANT MANAGER

Željko Buvač is a Bosnian football manager. He is the assistant manager of Premier League club Liverpool, a role he took in 2015 and still currently working with Jurgen Klopp.
